“数据粒”互联网迷因补完计划
设计师：杨立行 | 指导老师：魏弗兰

数据粒计划是关于中国互联网数据的补完计划。通过网络迷因的概念,将近年来的热门数据重新整理和 叙述,并用数据新闻的形式将它们重新定义。数据粒是一种假想的结构,是寄生于网络和人脑的信息元件 ,只有将其摆到台面上让它们不停地“发生”才能使其发挥功效,谨希望借此能提高人们的开放数据素养。

The Project Daticle is mean to make up and complete the Chinese Internet data. Re- neaten and re- narrate the hot data what came into being in the Chinese Internet recently years with the concept ”Meme”, and re- difine it with the method“data journalism”.“Daticle”is an imaginary structure, which you can regard as a imformation element parasitize the internet and human being’s brain. It can only be put into good use by making it being nascent , and we hope it can be conducive to enhance people’s open data literacy.
